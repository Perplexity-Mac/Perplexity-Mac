## Overview

<a href="#">Perplexity for Mac</a> extends its real-time search capabilities with a focus mode system, Perplexity Pages for publishing research, internal knowledge base search, and academic paper search that serve specialized research contexts where the standard web search approach needs to be focused on specific source types or organized for sharing with others.

The <a href="#">focus mode system</a> directs Perplexity's search to specific source categories rather than the general web. Academic mode searches scholarly papers and research publications for evidence-based academic answers with journal citations. YouTube mode finds and summarizes relevant video content. Reddit mode searches community discussions for user perspectives and real-world experiences. <a href="#">Wolfram Alpha integration</a> routes computational and mathematical queries to Wolfram Alpha's calculation engine, producing precise numerical answers, equation solutions, and data visualizations for quantitative questions that natural language AI models handle imprecisely.

<a href="#">Perplexity Pages</a> transforms research conducted in Perplexity into a formatted, shareable web page that presents the synthesized findings in a document-like layout with sections, citations, and images. A research thread exploring a complex topic becomes a publishable reference document — a competitive analysis, a technical overview, a market research report — hosted at a Perplexity URL that can be shared publicly or kept private. <a href="#">Pages editing</a> adds, removes, and restructures content in the generated document before publishing, applying the AI's research output as a draft that the author refines rather than a final product requiring no review.

<a href="#">Internal knowledge base search</a> in Perplexity for Enterprise connects the search engine to an organization's private documents, wikis, and data repositories, enabling AI-synthesized answers that draw from internal knowledge alongside or instead of public web sources. An employee asking Perplexity about company policy, internal processes, or product specifications receives answers synthesized from the organization's own documentation rather than generic web information. <a href="#">Collection sharing</a> makes Spaces collaborative — inviting team members to a shared Space enables collective research where multiple contributors add searches and documents that everyone in the Space can access and build upon.

## Key Features

- <a href="#">Academic focus mode</a> searches scholarly papers and research publications for evidence-based answers with journal-quality citations
- <a href="#">Reddit and YouTube focus modes</a> direct search to community discussions and video content for perspective-rich and visual research
- <a href="#">Wolfram Alpha integration</a> routes mathematical computational and quantitative queries to precise calculation engine responses
- <a href="#">Perplexity Pages publication</a> transforms research threads into formatted shareable web documents with sections citations and images
- <a href="#">Pages editing and refinement</a> adjusts AI-generated research documents before publishing for accurate polished final output
- <a href="#">Enterprise knowledge base search</a> connects to private organizational documents for AI-synthesized answers from internal sources
- <a href="#">Collaborative Spaces sharing</a> invites team members to shared research environments for collective knowledge accumulation
- <a href="#">Related questions suggestions</a> surfaces follow-up research directions based on answer content for systematic topic exploration
- <a href="#">Answer sharing and export</a> copies formatted answers with citations to clipboard or exports to document formats for downstream use

## Additional Information

The focus mode system reflects how research questions differ in their best source types — a medical question benefits from academic paper citations while a product question benefits from real user experiences in community discussions. Perplexity's ability to route different query types to the most appropriate sources makes it a more precise research tool than general web search, which returns mixed source types regardless of what the query actually needs.

Perplexity Pages addresses the last step in research workflows that AI tools have historically left incomplete — converting findings into a presentable format for sharing with others. The gap between AI-assisted research and deliverable output has required manual reformatting and document creation; Pages compresses this step by generating a publishable draft directly from the research conversation.
